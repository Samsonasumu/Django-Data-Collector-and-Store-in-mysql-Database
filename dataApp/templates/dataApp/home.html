ABOUT US

The Directorate of youth and sports is domiciled in the department of gender, > culture, youth, sports and social services. The Directorate is headed by a Director of Youth and sports. The Directorate coordinates all youth programs in the county including activities of NGO's dealing with youth. The Directorate also ensures there is a database on youth and youth programs, youth leadership framework and inclusion of youth in government.
