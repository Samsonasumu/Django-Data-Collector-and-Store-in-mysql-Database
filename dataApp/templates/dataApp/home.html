ABOUT US

The Directorate of youth and sports is domiciled in the Department of Gender, Culture, Youth, Sports and Social Services. The Directorate is headed by a Chief Officer of Youth and sports. The Directorate coordinates all youth and sports programs in the county. The Directorate also ensures there is a database on youth and youth programs, youth leadership framework and inclusion of youth in government.

Directorate Mandate

The Directorate of Youth and Sports is responsible for overseeing and supporting the development and well-being of the youth. This includes coordinating youth programs, collaborating with NGOs, maintaining a comprehensive youth database, and promoting youth talent and youth leadership.

Service Delivery Charter

We are committed to providing high-quality services to our clients. Our Service Delivery Charter outlines our promise to deliver excellence, transparency, and accountability in all our operations. We strive to meet and exceed your expectations by ensuring timely and efficient service delivery.

Mission, Vission and Achievements

MISSION

To promote youth development through talent nurturing and empowerment programmes

VISSION

To create a dynamic and empowerd society where all young people have access to transformative sports, personal growth and social economic empowerment

ACHIEVEMENTS

    Constitution and operationalisation of vihiga county youth service board
    Appointment and operationalisation of vihiga county sports management committee
    Support to local sports teams through vihiga county sports fund
    Training and capacity building of vihiga county youth on digital soft skills
